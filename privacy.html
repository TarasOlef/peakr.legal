PEAKR Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

PEAKR ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Taras Olefirenko, based in Spain. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the PEAKR mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account data: Your name and email address, obtained through Google Sign-In.
  • Health and wellness data: Sleep patterns and quality scores, exercise activity and intensity, stress levels, nutritional intake, and hormonal wellness indicators (energy, focus, mood, motivation, and libido levels as self-reported health biomarkers).
  • Usage data: App interaction data, feature usage, and session information for improving the app experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the PEAKR experience
  • To calculate your T-Score and track your wellness progress
  • To generate AI-powered health insights via our PRIME coach feature. Health data may be sent to OpenAI (openai.com) for analysis. OpenAI processes this data according to their privacy policy and does not use it to train their models.
  • To manage your subscription via RevenueCat (revenuecat.com)
  • To respond to support requests

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (supabase.com), a GDPR-compliant cloud infrastructure provider. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

4. GDPR Rights (EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at peakr.support@gmail.com

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

6. Third-Party Services

PEAKR uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In (authentication)
  • Supabase (data storage)
  • OpenAI (AI analysis features)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)

7. Children's Privacy

PEAKR is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately at peakr.support@gmail.com

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions:
